This project is a redesign of a biblical e-learning platform that works as a virtual classroom. The project began with the study of an existing platform using ux research techniques, the result of this research generated the need to redesign 2 things: the first, a landing page, from which students have access to the second: the new design of the e-learning platform.

Process details

Problem to solve: Most of the students do not find the exact route to enter the virtual class.

User Persona

Journey Map

Challenges identified

  • The platform is not user-friendly.
  • Most students get lost looking for options.
  • The platform does not have a hierarchical organization of information.
  • Users request telephone technical support a lot because the options are not intuitive.
  • The platform has no corporate identity.
  • The information on the platform is not presented uniformly.
