Landing page design for web and mobile

  • Project date: December, 2021
  • Country: Colombia

Promote a digital platform in a community of tenants and owners that is easy and safe to use, that scans the profiles and background research of people to validate that they are possible tenants.

Brief:

Company name: Weelo
Industry sector: Technology and Real Estate

Business model:
We are a community of tenants and owners with a 100% digital platform with experience in profile scanning and background investigation of people, achieving the best selection of tenants. We promote the payment of the rent through our platform with a points loyalty system and with an easy, safe and fast process.
We employ trained personnel, state-of-the-art technology and partners from bigger and more reliable data.

Product: Screen your tenant - Landing Page

Target audience of the landing: owners of real estate properties with the need to scan the profile of your potential tenant to make sure they are a trustworthy person to rent the property to.

What we want to achieve: Communicate through this landing that we are an easy and safe digital platform to use with a very complete result in the tenant profile report with more than 50 data to validate it.

Our personality: Clean, modern design and intuitive usability.
