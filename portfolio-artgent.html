About the company:
Argent is a startup that customizes high-end skateboards to suit its customers. It has been on the market for 6 months and is located in Colombia, in the cities of Bogotá and Medellín.

Target:
  • Young people between 15 and 28 years old
  • Male or female
  • People who have an urban style, have the means to buy high-end skateboards, describe themselves as rebellious, free and relaxed, belong and are part of the subculture associated with skateboarding, have a distinctive style within their friends and always seek to show that differentiation.
Purpose:
  • Customize and capture the voice of your customers on their skateboards.
  • Offer unique designs that stand out within the culture.
  • Show yourself as an unique brand
  • Sympathize with people in the enviroment
Sell ​​three types of custom skateboard services:
  • Basic: $ 50,000 (Unique design + printing of the same)
  • Medium: $ 125,000 (Unique design + 3 prints of it and set of wheels that match the design)
  • Premium: $ 150,000 (Unique design + 5 prints of it and set of wheels that match the design)
